20 Years and On: The Qatar Talks

The Qatar Talks is the annual flagship event of the International Competent Communicators (ICC-ONE), a non-profit organization founded on the principle of Community Social Responsibility (CSR). Since its inception, this national initiative has evolved from a competition into a vibrant platform that celebrates the art and science of communication.


Our mission is to nurture eloquence, creativity, and leadership among participants from diverse communities in Qatar.

Mohomed Ikhlas Farid

Founder

The journey, made possible by the vision and dedication of our founder, marks its 20th edition in 2025. Over two decades, The Qatar Talks has proudly noted the benefit to thousands of residents.
